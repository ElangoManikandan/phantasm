Tech Tornado
DESCRPTION

TechTornado is an exciting coding competition where creativity meets challenge! Solve fun and tricky problems, compete with brilliant minds, and showcase your coding skills. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, this is your chance to learn, grow, and win amazing prizes. Join the thrill—code, compete, and celebrate success!

RULES
  1. Complete all challenges within the given time.
  2. Submit your code before the deadline—late submissions won’t be accepted.
  3. Solutions will be evaluated based on accuracy, efficiency, and time complexity.
  4. Handle the provided system responsibly throughout the event.
